About Us

Google is a multinational technology company that focuses on various services and products that are related to the internet like online advertising, cloud computing, searching, hardware and software.
Google has been one of the first companies to really understand the need for employees to have a flexible schedule and work on their terms to unleash their creativity and a greater level of productivity. They've let their employees explore how they'd like to work and given them the freedom within the environment to approach work in a way that suits them.
Google has really teased out the most fun parts of working intech and amplified those to the maximum. It gives people a chance to solve huge problems while delivering people the most important resource they can have: information. They are a little irreverent with recreation and generous with perks. Those things always increase employee happiness, which has a direct relationship with productivity.
Google is just a fun place and doesn't feel like work. That is difficult to achieve in a company where employees often work long days and even weekends. However, with the perks, environment, and flexibility on where employees can work, Google has managed to create a very creative and enjoyable workplace.
You have to love a company that encourages you to bring your dog to work. Dogs are viewed as a way to enhance the quality of your work life. In the employee code of conduct, Google has written an entire section on dogs. Any company focused on the finer details that blur the distinction between the home and work environments is bound to get high marks on culture.
While Google's office perks are great, it’s not just the benefits that make а great culture. It’s something else: It’s the way management and teammates treat one another. That's what truly matters at the end of the day.
